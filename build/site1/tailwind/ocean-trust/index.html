Is there a warranty on Electrolux repairs?

Yes. We back our repair service with a warranty on labor and installed parts. We confirm the exact terms at the time of service.

Do you repair older Electrolux models?

Yes. We repair many older Electrolux appliances as long as parts remain available or compatible replacements exist.

What should I do before the technician arrives?

Clear access to the appliance, note symptoms and error codes, and locate your model/serial number. If it's a fridge/freezer issue, keep doors closed as much as possible. If you smell gas, turn it off (if safe) and ventilate the area.

How do I know if my appliance needs repair or replacement?

If the unit is under ~10 years old, has a single major issue, and the cabinet/structure is in good shape, repair usually makes sense. Replacement is more likely when there are repeated failures, severe sealed-system damage, heavy corrosion/leaks, or repair costs approach a large share of a new unit's price.
