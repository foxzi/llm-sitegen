## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does Electrolux appliance repair cost in ROSEVILLE?

### Do you use genuine Electrolux parts?

### Can I get same-day Electrolux repair?

### Is there a warranty on Electrolux repairs?
Yes. We back our repair service with a warranty on labor and installed parts. We confirm the exact terms at the time of service.

### Do you repair older Electrolux models?
Yes. We repair many older Electrolux appliances as long as parts remain available or compatible replacements exist.

### What should I do before the technician arrives?
Clear access to the appliance, note symptoms and error codes, and locate your model/serial number. If it's a fridge/freezer issue, keep doors closed as much as possible. If you smell gas, turn it off (if safe) and ventilate the area.

### How do I know if my Electrolux appliance needs repair or replacement?
If the unit is under ~10 years old, has a single major issue, and the cabinet/structure is in good shape, repair usually makes sense. Replacement is more likely when there are repeated failures, severe sealed-system damage, heavy corrosion/leaks, or repair costs approach a large share of a new unit's price.

### What do common Electrolux error codes mean?
They usually indicate a detected fault—often related to drainage, heating, temperature sensing, door/lock systems, or communication/control issues. The exact meaning varies by model, so we confirm it using your model number and proper diagnostics before replacing anything.

### Are maintenance plans available?
Yes. We offer maintenance options upon request to help reduce breakdowns and extend the life of your Electrolux appliance.

### Can you service Electrolux appliances still under manufacturer warranty?
We can inspect and diagnose them. For covered repairs, the manufacturer may require an authorized warranty provider; we'll explain the best next step after we verify your warranty status and model.

### What payment methods are accepted?
Most customers can pay by credit/debit card, cash, or electronic payment. If you have a preferred method, mention it when booking and we'll confirm availability.
